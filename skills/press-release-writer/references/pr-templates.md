# Press Release Templates by Announcement Type

Use these as starting scaffolds. Customize every bracket. Never publish a template verbatim.

---

## Template 1 — Funding Round

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

[Company] Raises $[X]M [Series] to [Solve Specific Problem] as [Market Trend] Reaches Inflection Point
[Subheadline: What the funding enables and for which customer segment]

[CITY, Date] — [Company], the [brief category descriptor] for [ICP], today announced it has raised $[X] million in [Series] funding led by [Lead Investor], with participation from [Co-investors]. The round brings total funding to $[X]M and will be used to [use of funds: 3 specific items].

The fundraise comes as [market trend or macro condition] has created [specific, quantified problem] for [customer type]. [Company]'s [product/platform] [specific mechanism of action], enabling [customer type] to [specific outcome] — [without / instead of / compared to] [status quo or alternative].

"[Market or strategic thesis statement — not a product description]," said [Investor Name], [Title] at [Firm]. "[One sentence connecting the investment thesis to the company's traction or differentiation]."

Since [founding / last raise / product launch], [Company] has [growth metric: ARR, customers, usage], including [named customer logos if available]. [One additional proof point that signals momentum].

"[CEO's strategic statement about why this moment in the market demands this company]," said [CEO Name], CEO of [Company]. "[One sentence on what the company will look like in 18–24 months with this capital]."

The $[X]M round will accelerate [specific initiative 1], expand [specific initiative 2], and fund [specific initiative 3] — with a particular focus on [key geography or vertical].

[Company] is [accepting applications / available in beta / generally available] to [customer type]. [For more information / To request access], visit [URL].

###

About [Company]
[Company] [does what] for [who], [how it's differentiated from alternatives]. Founded in [year] and headquartered in [city], [Company] serves [N] customers including [named logos]. Learn more at [URL].

About [Lead Investor Firm]
[2 sentences on the VC firm.] Learn more at [investor URL].

Media Contact:
[Name] | [Title] | [Email] | [Phone]
```

## Template 2 — Product Launch

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

[Company] Launches [Product Name], [Specific Outcome] for [ICP] in [Timeframe]
[Subheadline: The key differentiator and availability date]

[CITY, Date] — [Company], [brief descriptor], today launched [Product Name], a [product category] that [specific mechanism] to help [ICP] [specific outcome]. [Product Name] is [generally available / in limited beta / available by invitation] starting [date].

[The problem paragraph: describe the status quo pain with specifics. What do customers do today? How long does it take? What does it cost? What breaks?]

[Product Name] addresses this by [mechanism of action — how it works in plain language]. Unlike [category alternative], [Product Name] [key differentiator in one sentence with a specific, measurable claim].

"[Customer outcome in their own words — must include a specific number or timeframe]," said [Customer Name], [Title] at [Customer Company]. "[One sentence on what they were doing before and what changed]."

Key capabilities of [Product Name] include:
- [Capability 1]: [What it does and the benefit]
- [Capability 2]: [What it does and the benefit]
- [Capability 3]: [What it does and the benefit]

[Product Name] integrates with [key integrations] and is available on [platforms/OS/cloud]. Pricing starts at $[X]/[unit] with [free tier / trial / enterprise options] available.

"[CEO or CPO quote: the market thesis that makes this product necessary right now]," said [Name], [Title] at [Company]. "[One sentence on where this product fits in the company's broader platform vision]."

To learn more or request access, visit [URL].

About [Company]
[3-sentence boilerplate]

[Name] | [Title] | [Email] | [Phone]
```

## Template 3 — Partnership or Integration

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

[Company A] and [Company B] Partner to [Specific Outcome] for [Shared Customer Segment]
[Subheadline: What each company brings and the combined benefit]

[CITY, Date] — [Company A] and [Company B] today announced a [strategic / technology / go-to-market] partnership that [specific outcome for joint customers]. The partnership enables [customer type] to [combined benefit] without [the friction or cost of the status quo].

[The market problem paragraph: why this partnership is necessary now. What gap existed that neither company could solve alone?]

Through the partnership, [Company A]'s [product/platform] will [specific integration or capability] with [Company B]'s [product/platform], enabling joint customers to [specific workflow or outcome that was not previously possible].

"[Quote from a joint customer or a named pilot customer using the integration — must include a specific outcome metric]," said [Customer Name], [Title] at [Customer Company].

"[Company A exec quote: why this partner, why now, and what it unlocks for customers]," said [Name], [Title] at [Company A]. "[One sentence on the company's broader partnership strategy or platform vision]."

"[Company B exec quote: why this partnership fits their strategy and what it delivers for shared customers]," said [Name], [Title] at [Company B]. "[One sentence on the scale or reach this partnership adds]."

The integration is [available now / in beta / generally available on date] to customers of [Company A] and [Company B]. [Details on how to access: URL, support resources, etc.]

###

About [Company A]
[3-sentence boilerplate]

About [Company B]
[3-sentence boilerplate]

Media Contact — [Company A]:
[Name] | [Title] | [Email] | [Phone]

Media Contact — [Company B]:
[Name] | [Title] | [Email] | [Phone]
```

## Template 4 — Executive Hire

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

[Company] Appoints [Name] as [Title] to [Strategic Goal]
[Subheadline: What they built or led before and why it matters here]

[CITY, Date] — [Company] today announced the appointment of [Full Name] as [Title]. [Name] brings [N] years of experience [specific achievement or background most relevant to the role] and will [lead / oversee / build] [function or initiative] at [Company].

[The growth context paragraph: what stage the company is at and why this hire is strategically timed. Revenue, headcount, or market expansion signals that make this role necessary now.]

[Name] most recently served as [Previous Title] at [Previous Company], where [specific achievement in numbers: team built, revenue grown, product launched, company sold]. Before that, [1 sentence on prior relevant role].

"[Exec hire's own quote: what drew them to this company, what they plan to build, and the market opportunity they see]," said [Full Name]. "[One sentence on their specific 90-day or 6-month priority]."

"[CEO quote: why this person, why now, and what this hire signals about company direction]," said [CEO Name], CEO of [Company]. "[One sentence on the company's roadmap or growth phase]."

[Name] holds [degree / credentials] from [institution] and [optional: board positions, advisory roles, or published work].
```

## Template 5 — Customer Milestone / Growth Announcement

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

[Company] Surpasses [Milestone: X customers / $XM ARR / X users] as [Market Shift] Drives Adoption
[Subheadline: Rate of growth and what's driving it]

[CITY, Date] — [Company], the [descriptor] for [ICP], today announced it has surpassed [specific milestone], reaching [metric] [faster than any previous benchmark / in less time than / ahead of original targets]. The milestone represents [growth rate: X% YoY / X× in Y months] and signals [broader market trend].

[Context paragraph: what is driving this growth. Is it a macro trend? A new use case emerging? A specific customer segment accelerating adoption? Be specific — not "customers love our product" but "mid-market fintech companies accelerated adoption following [specific regulation / event / catalyst]."]

[Company]'s customers include [named logos or category descriptors] across [industries or geographies], with [X%] of new customers coming from [specific segment] in [timeframe].

"[Customer quote: specific outcome with a number, not a compliment]," said [Customer Name], [Title] at [Customer Company]. "[One sentence on what they were doing before and why they chose this solution]."

"[CEO quote: what this milestone signals about the market and where the company goes from here]," said [CEO Name], CEO of [Company]. "[One sentence on investment in growth or next phase of the product]."

To learn more, visit [URL].
```

## Template 6 — Original Research / Data Release

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

New [Company] Research: [Surprising Finding] — [N]% of [Audience] [Counterintuitive Behavior or Outcome]
[Subheadline: Who was studied and why the finding matters to practitioners]

[CITY, Date] — [Company] today published [Report Title], a study of [N] [subjects — companies, practitioners, transactions, etc.] that reveals [key finding in plain language]. The research, conducted [methodology summary: how, when, and from whom], found [supporting finding] and [third finding].

[The significance paragraph: why this finding matters to the reader's job or business. What decisions does this change? What conventional wisdom does it overturn?]

Key findings include:
- [Finding 1 with specific percentage or number]
- [Finding 2 with specific percentage or number]
- [Finding 3 with specific percentage or number]
- [Finding 4 with specific percentage or number — the most counterintuitive one]

"[Practitioner quote from the research: an interview subject or reference customer who embodies the finding]," said [Name], [Title] at [Organization].

"[Company spokesperson quote: what this research means for how [ICP] should be operating or investing]," said [Name], [Title] at [Company]. "[One sentence on how the company's product or approach addresses the finding]."

The full report, including methodology, regional breakdowns, and industry-specific data, is available at [URL].

About [Company]
[3-sentence boilerplate]

Media Contact:
[Name] | [Title] | [Email] | [Phone]
```
