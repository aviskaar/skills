## Template 2 — Quarterly Board Pack Structure

### Slide 1: Executive Summary
- Revenue vs. plan (waterfall chart)
- Cash position and runway chart
- Top 3 priorities for next quarter
- Any governance matters

### Slide 2: Revenue Deep Dive
- ARR waterfall: Beginning + New + Expansion − Contraction − Churn = Ending ARR
- Revenue by segment/product
- New customer count, average contract value, win rate
- Pipeline coverage ratio

### Slide 3: P&L vs. Budget
- Side-by-side: Budget / Actuals / Variance / Variance %
- Gross margin bridge (vs. prior quarter and budget)
- Key opex drivers — hiring-driven vs. program spend

### Slide 4: Cash Flow & Runway
- 13-week cash flow forecast (weekly bars)
- Runway under base / downside / upside scenarios
- Planned financing events (if any)

### Slide 5: Unit Economics
- CAC by channel (blended and by top 3 channels)
- LTV:CAC ratio (target: ≥ 3:1)
- Payback period (target: ≤ 18 months)
- NRR (target: ≥ 110%)
- Burn multiple (target: < 1.5)

### Slide 6: Headcount & People
- Actual vs. plan headcount (by department)
- Open roles and expected hire dates
- Attrition rate (voluntary vs. involuntary)
- Compensation cost per employee trend

### Slide 7: Forward Guidance
- Updated full-year forecast (revenue, burn, cash)
- Key assumptions and sensitivities
- Risks to plan (top 3, with mitigation)

## Template 3 — Variance Analysis Narrative

For each P&L line with variance > 10% or > $10,000:

```
Account: [Account Name]
Budget: $[X]  |  Actual: $[X]  |  Variance: $[X] ([%])

Root Cause:
  [Primary driver — 1-2 sentences explaining exactly why the variance occurred]

Nature of Variance:
  ☐ Timing difference (will recover in [month])
  ☐ Permanent overspend (budget amendment needed)
  ☐ Permanent underspend (reallocate to [category])
  ☐ One-time item (non-recurring)

Management Action:
  [What CFO / department head is doing about it]

Revised Full-Year Impact:
  [Full-year budget: $X → Updated forecast: $X → Change: $X]
```
